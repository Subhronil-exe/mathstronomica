MATHSTRONOMICA

Astrophysics + Mathematics — live satellite & orbit model for exhibitions and education

Project Overview

Mathstronomica is a student-built demonstration that combines maths and astrophysics: visualising satellite motion, measuring distances with ultrasonic sensors, monitoring temperature, and demonstrating automated tracking using motors. The system is designed to be reliable at exhibitions and accessible for classroom learning.

Equipment List

  • Wires (assorted)—

How It Works

The ultrasonic sensor measures distance; the Arduino processes data to estimate position and speed. When a satellite model passes, motors rotate to simulate tracking. Temperature sensing and the rheostat ensure safe demos and adjustable motor speed during exhibitions.

Future Upgrades
